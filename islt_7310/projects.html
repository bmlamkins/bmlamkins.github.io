Digital Photo Project

This project was completed in Introduction to Digital Media. The requirements for this project were that students used Adobe Photoshop to implement beginner and advanced raster and vector techniques.


Competencies:

1. Students will develop skills for social responsible technology leadership

Through this project, I was able to use new photo editing skills to create a learning graphic that will enhance learning in the classroom, and that will allow me to share with my co-workers..


2. Students will design and develop learning systems

This project allowed me to use Adobe Photoshop to develop a learning graphic to enhance student learning outcomes.

Digital Video Project

The goal for this project was to create an original video between 30-90 seconds that included separate audio tracks, a title screen, credits, two shots, two angles, and two transitions in order to support a learning objective.


Competencies:

1. Students will develop skills for social responsible technology leadership

This project allowed me to record and edit an original video to enhance a learning objective that will enhance learning in the classroom, and that will allow me to share with my co-workers.


2. Students will design and develop learning systems

I utlizied Adobe Premiere Pro, Audition, and Photoshop in order to create a video to enhance a learning objective.
